HARRY POTTER AND THE CURSED CHILD


THE BOOK

HARRY POTTER AND THE CURSED CHILD is a two-part stage play, based on the Harry Potter series by J.K. Rowling

AUTHOR OF THE BOOK

J.K. Rowling is a British author best known for creating the Harry Potter series, one of the most successful literary franchises in history. Her books have been translated into numerous languages and adapted into blockbuster films, making her one of the wealthiest and most influential authors of her time. Rowling is also recognized for her philanthropy and activism, particularly in support of children's welfare and social justice causes.
